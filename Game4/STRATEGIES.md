## Beginner Tips (Score: 50-150)

### Master the Basics
1. **Start Slow**: Take your time in early levels to understand game mechanics
2. **Plan Routes**: Think 2-3 moves ahead before turning
3. **Avoid Walls Early**: Corners are tricky - stay in the middle initially
4. **Vertical vs Horizontal**: Practice moving smoothly in both directions
5. **Get Comfortable**: Play for 5-10 minutes to warm up

### Control Mastery
1. **Use Arrow Keys**: More responsive than WASD for some keyboards
2. **Quick Reflexes**: Press keys decisively, not slowly
3. **Anticipate Changes**: Predict food spawns and plan accordingly
4. **Smooth Turns**: Avoid sudden 90-degree turns when possible
5. **Space Management**: Keep worm centered to maximize maneuverability

### Scoring Basics
1. **Regular Food Focus**: Consistently collect red food first
2. **Early Level Advantage**: Easier to rack up points at lower speeds
3. **Combo Momentum**: Build length early for better control later
4. **Safe Strategies**: Prioritize survival over aggressive plays
